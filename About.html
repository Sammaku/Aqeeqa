virtues of Aeeqa


The Aqiqah is a confirmed Sunnah in Islam. It is the sacrifice made for a newborn on the seventh day after birth, and it carries significant virtue in Islamic teachings. The virtues of the Aqiqah include:

  • 1. Thanking Allah for the blessing: The Aqiqah is an expression of gratitude to Allah for the blessing of a newborn child.

  • 2. Drawing closer to Allah: By offering the Aqiqah, a person is drawing closer to Allah by following the teachings of the Prophet.

  • 3. Protection for the child: According to some hadiths, the Aqiqah protects the child from harm and the influence of Satan.

  • 4. Spreading joy and sharing: It is recommended to distribute the meat of the Aqiqah to family, friends, and the needy, fostering a sense of community and love among people.

  • 5. Forgiveness of sins: In some narrations, it is mentioned that the Aqiqah can be a means for the forgiveness of sins and the removal of hardship.

    • The Sunnah is to sacrifice two sheep for a boy and one for a girl, but this is not obligatory. If someone cannot afford two, they may offer one even for a boy.
